Honda Acty

Key Specs

Engine656cc I3
DriveRWD / 4WD
Transmission5-speed manual
Payload350 kg
Fuel Economy16–19 km/l

Description

The Honda Acty was produced from 1977 to 2021 and became popular for its efficiency and long-lasting engines. It is one of the most common Kei trucks exported to the United States today, especially the 1990s models.
